Artist Submissions

Interested in exhibiting at Orchard Galleries? We review artist submissions on a rolling basis. Please include your artist statement, CV, and portfolio images.

Private Viewings

Schedule a private tour outside regular hours. Perfect for collectors, corporate groups, or anyone seeking a more intimate gallery experience.

Email: appointments@orchardgalleries.com

Gallery Rentals
