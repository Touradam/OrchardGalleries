Reflections in Color

March 15 - May 30, 2026

Experience the vibrant world of contemporary abstract expressionism through the works of five pioneering Bay Area artists. This groundbreaking exhibition explores the intersection of color theory, emotion, and spatial relationships in modern art.

Featuring works by Sarah Chen, Marcus Williams, Elena Rodriguez, David Park, and Kenji Tanaka, "Reflections in Color" invites visitors to immerse themselves in bold palettes and dynamic compositions that challenge traditional perceptions of abstract art.

About Orchard Galleries

Located in the heart of Oakland's vibrant Art Murmur District, Orchard Galleries is a contemporary art space dedicated to fostering community engagement while highlighting the extraordinary diversity and creativity of Bay Area artists.

Our 12 exhibition showrooms host rotating exhibitions, installations, artist showcases, community events, open studios, and cultural programming that bring together artists, collectors, and art enthusiasts in a welcoming, inspiring environment.

We believe art has the power to transform communities, spark conversations, and build bridges between cultures. Through our carefully curated exhibitions and inclusive programming, we strive to make contemporary art accessible and engaging for everyone.
