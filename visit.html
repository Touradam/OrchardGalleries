Getting Here

By Car

Orchard Galleries is located in Oakland's Art Murmur District, easily accessible from major Bay Area highways.

From San Francisco: Take I-80 East across the Bay Bridge, exit at Grand Avenue, continue to 25th Street. Gallery is on the right.

From Berkeley: Take I-80 South, exit at Grand Avenue, turn right on 25th Street.

From South Bay: Take I-880 North, exit at 23rd Avenue, turn left on International Blvd, right on 25th Street.

Parking

Street parking is available on 25th Street and surrounding blocks. Metered parking available Monday-Saturday 9 AM - 6 PM. Free on Sundays. A public parking lot is located at Telegraph Avenue and 27th Street, a short walk from the gallery.

By Public Transportation

BART: Take BART to 19th Street Oakland station. Walk or take AC Transit bus to 25th Street (approximately 15-minute walk or 5-minute bus ride).

AC Transit: Multiple bus lines serve the area including the 1, 12, 51A, and NL. Check AC Transit website for current schedules and routes.

By Bike

Oakland is a bike-friendly city. Bike racks are available directly in front of the gallery on 25th Street.

Visitor Information

Admission

Admission to Orchard Galleries is always free. We believe art should be accessible to everyone in our community.

Accessibility

Orchard Galleries is wheelchair accessible with ramped entrances and an elevator to all floors. Accessible restrooms are available. If you have specific accessibility needs or questions, please contact us in advance at (510) 555-ARTS or info@orchardgalleries.com.

Photography

Personal photography is welcome for non-commercial use. Please be respectful of other visitors and do not use flash photography. Some special exhibitions may have photography restrictions, which will be clearly posted.

Group Visits

We welcome group visits from schools, universities, art organizations, and community groups. To schedule a group visit or arrange for a guided tour, please contact us at least two weeks in advance.

What to Expect

Plan to spend 45-90 minutes exploring our 12 exhibition showrooms. Our galleries feature contemporary art in various media including painting, sculpture, installation, photography, and mixed media. Seating areas are available throughout the space for contemplation and rest.

Gallery Etiquette

To ensure an enjoyable experience for all visitors:

  • Please do not touch the artwork
  • Maintain a respectful volume when conversing
  • Food and drinks are not permitted in gallery spaces
  • Large bags and backpacks should be checked at the front desk
  • Children must be supervised at all times

Explore the Neighborhood

Make a day of it in Oakland's vibrant Art Murmur District

Art Galleries

The Art Murmur District is home to dozens of galleries and artist studios. Pick up a neighborhood map at our front desk to explore the area.

Restaurants & Cafes

Telegraph Avenue and surrounding streets feature diverse dining options from casual cafes to fine dining, representing Oakland's multicultural character.

Shopping

Discover local boutiques, vintage shops, bookstores, and maker spaces featuring work by Bay Area artisans and designers.

First Friday

On the first Friday of each month, the entire district comes alive with extended gallery hours, street performances, food vendors, and special events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is admission really free?

Yes! Admission to our exhibitions is always free. Some special workshops and programs may have fees, which are clearly indicated.

Do I need to make a reservation?

No reservation is needed for regular Saturday hours or First Friday events. Private viewings during the week require advance booking.

Can I purchase artwork?

Yes, much of the artwork on display is available for purchase. Gallery staff can provide pricing information and assist with acquisitions.

Are guided tours available?

We offer curator-led tours on select Saturdays (check our events calendar). Private group tours can be arranged by appointment.

Is the gallery family-friendly?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors of all ages. We also host special Family Art Days with activities designed for children.

Can I bring my service animal?

Yes, service animals are always welcome at Orchard Galleries.
