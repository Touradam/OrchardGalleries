Exhibition Programs

Each exhibition at Orchard Galleries is accompanied by a rich program of events designed to deepen engagement with the artwork and artists. Our exhibition programs typically include:

Artist Talks & Panel Discussions

Hear directly from artists about their creative processes, inspirations, and the ideas behind their work. Panel discussions bring together multiple voices to explore exhibition themes.

Guided Tours

Join our curators for in-depth tours that provide context, highlight key works, and offer insights into curatorial decisions. Tours are free with gallery admission.

Workshops

Hands-on workshops led by exhibiting artists give participants the opportunity to explore techniques and materials firsthand.

Opening Receptions

Celebrate new exhibitions with the artists and fellow art enthusiasts. Our opening receptions feature light refreshments and provide wonderful opportunities for conversation.
