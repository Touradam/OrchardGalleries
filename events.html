Event Types

We host a diverse range of programs designed to engage, educate, and inspire

Opening Receptions

Celebrate new exhibitions with artists, curators, and fellow art enthusiasts. Opening receptions typically include artist remarks, gallery tours, and light refreshments.

Artist Talks

Hear directly from exhibiting artists about their work, inspiration, and creative processes. These intimate conversations offer deeper engagement with the artwork.

Workshops

Hands-on learning experiences led by professional artists. Workshops cover various techniques and media, welcoming participants of all skill levels.

Panel Discussions

Thought-provoking conversations bringing together multiple voices to explore themes related to art, culture, and community.

Gallery Tours

Curator-led tours provide context, highlight key works, and offer insights into exhibition themes and artistic practices.

Community Events

Special programs including Family Art Days, First Fridays, Open Studios, and cultural celebrations that bring the community together.

Private Events & Gallery Rentals

Orchard Galleries offers a stunning, sophisticated setting for private events, corporate gatherings, and special celebrations. Our versatile spaces can accommodate intimate receptions or larger gatherings surrounded by world-class contemporary art.

Available for:

  • Corporate events and receptions
  • Private parties and celebrations
  • Fundraisers and benefits
  • Photo shoots and film productions
